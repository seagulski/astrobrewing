Born from Stardust

At Astro Brewing Company, we believe that great beer is like the universe itself – complex, beautiful, and full of endless possibilities. Founded in 2018 by a craft beer lover.

Our garage brewery combines cutting-edge brewing technology with time-honored techniques, creating beers that are truly astronomical. Every batch is crafted with precision, passion, and a touch of cosmic inspiration.

From hoppy IPAs that shine like distant stars to dark stouts as mysterious as black holes, each beer in our lineup tells a story of exploration and discovery.
